## Communication Principles

1. **Timeliness**: Communicate early and often. Do not wait until the next scheduled update to raise risks or blockers.
2. **Clarity**: Use plain language. Avoid jargon unless communicating with a technical audience.
3. **Consistency**: Use the templates above to ensure a consistent format and tone.
4. **Single Source of Truth**: Link to the project board, risk register, or release notes rather than duplicating information.
5. **Audience Awareness**: Tailor the level of detail and technical depth to the intended audience.
6. **Feedback Loops**: Actively solicit feedback from stakeholders and adjust communication approach as needed.
